Easy Installation

Get up and running in just a few simple steps

1

Upload the Plugin

Upload the vertical-menu-with-images folder to your WordPress plugins directory.

2

Activate

Go to your WordPress admin panel and activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu.

3

Configure Your Menu

Create or edit an existing WordPress menu under Appearance > Menus and add images to your menu items.

4

Add to Your Site

Use the shortcode [vertical_menu_with_images] or the Elementor widget to display your menu anywhere.
