Who I Am

Full-Stack Developer & Problem Solver

Hello! I'm Nayan Ray, a passionate full-stack developer specializing in WordPress, Laravel, and modern JavaScript frameworks. With over 7 years of experience, I transform complex ideas into elegant digital solutions.

My journey began in Khulna, Bangladesh, where I developed my first website at age 16. Since then, I've worked with clients worldwide, delivering projects that range from e-commerce platforms to custom web applications.

I believe in clean, efficient code and user-centered design. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring aspiring developers.
